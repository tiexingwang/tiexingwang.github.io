Tiexing Wang

Affiliations. R&D Shearwater Geoservices. United Kingdom.

Tiexing Wang is a research geophysicist on the R&D team at Shearwater GeoServices, United Kingdom.

His main research areas are physics-ML and signal processing. He is particularly focused on building a physics-aware framework that eliminates the need for supervised learning with ground truth data. This framework aims to be a reliable solver for inverse problems.

He is also a board member of the EAGE London Local Chapter and the EAGE Young Professionals Committee, where he promotes scientific research and addresses scientific challenges during the energy transition.
